AI-augmented workflow

How I work with AI

Both sides of the AI story: I design AI-driven products people trust — and I run my own daily workflow on AI tools.

In-repo prototyping with Claude Code

I prototype and inspect UI directly in the product repository — testing feasibility in real code, so design decisions land dev-ready and handoff friction disappears.

Concept exploration at speed

Lovable and Figma Make for rapid first-pass concepts — exploring more directions before committing, especially in early discovery.

AI as a design critic

I pressure-test flows, information architecture, and accessibility with AI critiques before stakeholder reviews — catching issues while they're cheap to fix.

Research & multi-market communication

AI-assisted research synthesis and translation keep multi-market work — US, Germany, France, Poland — fast and precise.

Wider exploration, earlier validation, and a tighter design–development loop — the workflow behind leading a full enterprise platform redesign as the sole designer.
